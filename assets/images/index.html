About ICE Technologies

ICE Technologies began its journey in 2003, with a group of young engineers under the guidance of senior project managers. It started to provide systems Integration (SI) services to the growing business community in Bangladesh. As a niche SI, our primary focus was on the ERP, telecom billing and customer care solutions.

Today along with SI projects ICE Technologies also focus on innovative technology and infrastructure project.

Airtime Recharge

Immigration is a global phenomenon. As potential migrants discover higher paying jobs and lower costs of living, they are drawn to and from nearly every nation on earth. At Ice Tech we have always focused on providing communication backbone solutions for the betterment of the users and global community. With this aim in mind we have set up our carrier grade platform with dedicated call center support to provide international airtime remittance termination to all the mobile operators in Bangladesh.

ICE Tech is also providing domestic airtime topup services to corporate customers in Bangladesh. With visibility in bill collection and payment ICE Tech airtime recharge platform is a one-stop-solution for corporates to manage their entire mobile topup services.
